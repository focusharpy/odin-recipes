Easy Roasted Broccoli

Description

Easy to make roasted broccoli that is much more flavorsome than boiled.

Below you will find a list of ingredients and easy to follow step-by-step instructions.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Broccoli Head
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees F
  2. Cut the florets from the stalk.
  3. Peel the stalk and cut into slices.
  4. Toss the florets and stem pieces with oil, then trasnfer to a baking sheet.
  5. Season with salt and pepper and roast in the preheated oven for 20 minutes.
